Welcome to Hotel Amador!

The Hotel Amador provides luxury lodging in the heart of Chinandega. The hotel provides views of the beautiful mountains of Nicaragua and the breathtaking volcanos that surround Chinandega. The hotel is walking distance from local markets, malls, and museums.

  • Enjoy the rooftop garden restaurants while sulking in the view of Nicaragua's landscape
  • Walk to the local markets and embrace handmade crafts made by locals
  • Take a tour of the famous Flor de Cana © factory and sample some of their famous rum
  • Take a hike on one of the gorgeous mountains and enjoy a panoramic view of the city
